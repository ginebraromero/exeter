About EXETER

EXETER is Bladee's third studio album and eleventh project. It is executively produced by Gud and contains features from Ecco2k, whom are both members of Drain Gang along with Bladee. It was released on 8 April 2020.


Bladee described the project to Spotify the day of release:

Me and Gud recorded this album during a one week visit to Gotland island this last summer. The inspiration for me was trying to paint and express an inner image or feeling that I have through sound. The album for me is a kind of practice of focus and play in the incorporeal.

It was also described by label YEAR0001 on social media:

Bladee's latest project 'Exeter' was written and recorded during a week-long stay in a remote house on the Swedish island of Gotland - a barren and austere setting which in numerous ways came to impact the soundscape of it's 9 Gud-produced tracks.

WONDERLAND was previewed by Gud on Instagram on 14 August 2019. EVERY MOMENT SPECIAL, DNA RAIN, and LOVESTORY were also previewed by Bladee and Gud prior to their release. The album title, tracklist, artwork, and label surfaced on music-recognition software Shazam on 5 April 2020.


Pitchfork rated EXETER with a 7.2/10, commented:

The enigmatic Swedish rapper strips back his hypnotic music until it's nothing but AutoTuned crooning and chanted slogans. [...] Bladee's music has always been about building alternate universes, [...] it's the sort of album that rewards you the closer you lean into it.

Bladee's October 2020 Light in Light tour was announced along with the project's release.
